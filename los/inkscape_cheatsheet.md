```

# Legend

LC = Left Click
RC = Richt Click
DC = Double Click
WC = Wheel Click
WU = Wheel scroll Up
WD = Wheel scroll Down
MD = Mouse Drag

# Favorites

Ctrl + B                    - Show scroll bars
WC + MD                     - Move canvas
Ctrl + WU                   - Zoom in
Ctrl + WD                   - Zoom out

1                           - Zoom 1:1
3                           - Zoom selection
5                           - Zoom page

Q                           - Zoom 2:1
Q                           - Fill screen with selection









# Space + MD                - # Move canvas
WU                          - Move canvas up
WD                          - Move canvas down
Shift + WU                  - Move canvas left
Shift + MD                  - Move canvas right
```
